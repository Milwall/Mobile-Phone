Who are we?

Checkout the Team Profiles here.

The MobilePhone Company was founded in 2014 by 4 students at the National College of Ireland with an idea of developing a different way to get a new phone online creating a unique market position by expanding the range of products and services to meet the demands of a changing market.

In Ireland, the first MobilePhone shop began trading on Dublin's Nassau Street, since then over 10 stores have opened nationwide being present in every major town and city in Ireland.

Among our commitments to our customers, we offer:

  • Variety with the widest range of mobile phones.
  • Expert, simple and unbiased advice.
  • The best price guarantee.
